ABOUT HONEY

our
story

In the late 50s Esra shared beekeeping with his son, Richard. With increased demand, they founded Barkman Honey Company in 1960

Their flagship brand, Busy Bee, epitomized nature's finest, delighting customers with each bottle.

Esra Barkman's passion for bees led him from Kansas to Oregon in the 1920s, where he honed his beekeeping skills.

our
misson

Bee health initiatives include bee and environmental research as well as sustainable farming practices.

Habitat preservation helps ensure that bees have the environment they need not just to survive – but to thrive in harmony.

Beekeeper recruitment helps build a solid base of next-generation bee advocates and honey producers.

Consumer education boosts awareness of the value of pollination and the plight of the honeybee.
